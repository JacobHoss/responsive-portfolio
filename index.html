About Me

Where to start? My name is Jacob. I was born and raised in Metro Atlanta- Marietta, to be exact. I've spent most of my life in school, in one form or another. Going to Kennesaw State University, I pursued an education in Business, with a degree in Entrepreneurship. What drew me to that course was the optimistic mindset of entrepreneurs.

During school, I got my license to be a Georgia Insurance Agent. Ever since, I have worked for State Farm Agents, managing policyholder portfolios. I was made privy to Coding Boot Camps from word-of-mouth. I came to the conclusion that they present me an excellent opportunity. As of June 23, 2020, I am a few weeks into the GA Tech Full Stack Development Boot Camp. It's incredible to think how much I have learned in such a short time. It has been intimidating, yet exciting. I am anxious to see what the remainder of the course holds.
